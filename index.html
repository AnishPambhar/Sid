Our architects breathe life into dreams, creating environments where innovation meets tradition, and spaces transcend mere structures

SID Architects, established in 2018 by Harshid Pambhar, is a Surat-based firm specializing in a diverse range of projects, including commercial buildings, residential designs, factories, and interior works for offices, jewelry shops, and showrooms. We are dedicated to innovative minimalist designs that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal, ensuring client satisfaction across all project types.

PRINCIPAL ARCHITECT

Harshid Pambhar

Harshid Pambhar's design philosophy centers on the principles of minimalist architecture, emphasizing the harmony between form and function. He believes that great design should not only be visually appealing but also sustainable and budget-friendly. By prioritizing simplicity and clarity, Harshid creates spaces that evoke a sense of tranquility while meeting the diverse needs of clients. His approach challenges conventional notions of architecture, demonstrating that exceptional design can be both accessible and impactful.
